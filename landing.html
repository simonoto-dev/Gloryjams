How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

No signups, no paywalls, no friction between you and playing music tonight.

1
Search your city

Type in your location. We use Google Places to find jam sessions, open mics, and musician meetups happening nearby.

2
Pick a session

See dates, times, venues, genres. Read what other musicians have said. Find the one that fits your vibe and your schedule.

3
Show up and play

Check in so others know you're going. Bring your instrument. That's it. No cover, no audition, no barriers.
